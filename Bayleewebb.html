My Puppy Bindi

My name is Baylee Webb and I have an amazing, sweet puppy named Bindi. She is just seven months old and the best dog I have ever had. I got her in June when she was eight weeks old and barely nine pounds.I got her at the Athens Humane Society; I definetely did not expect to come home wioth such a young puppy when I went that day, but five months later and I love her more than anything!

I was not was also not prepared for the life change I was in store for. Taking care of a puppy alone is no easy feat, but getting a companion who will love me no matter what makes it all worthwhile. She has definitely come a long way since those first few days and now even my parents compliment me on how well she is trained!
